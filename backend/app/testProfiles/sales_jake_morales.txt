Jake Morales is a Regional Sales Manager at CloudBridge IT Solutions based in Denver Colorado where he has been working since 2020. He has about 10 years of experience in B2B technology sales primarily selling SaaS and cloud infrastructure solutions to mid-market companies in the financial services and healthcare industries. Before CloudBridge he worked at Salesforce for three years as an account executive covering the Rocky Mountain region and before that he was at a small cybersecurity startup in Boulder called ShieldNet where he cut his teeth doing cold outreach and building a book of business from scratch. He graduated from Colorado State University in 2016 with a degree in Marketing and a minor in Computer Information Systems. He describes himself on LinkedIn as someone who believes sales is about solving problems not pushing products and says his best client relationships started with genuine curiosity about their business challenges. He is a big Denver Broncos and Denver Nuggets fan and has had Nuggets season tickets since 2022. On weekends he plays in a recreational soccer league in the Denver area and also enjoys hiking with his dog a golden retriever named Cashflow which he posts about frequently on Instagram. He volunteers as a mentor with the Denver chapter of SCORE helping small business owners with go-to-market strategy. He recently attended the SaaStr Annual conference in the Bay Area and posted about trends in vertical SaaS for financial services. His company CloudBridge IT Solutions has around 150 employees and focuses on helping companies migrate legacy systems to cloud infrastructure with a specialization in compliance heavy industries like banking and healthcare across the western United States.
